It also allows me to go somewhere by foot and then come back by bike or vice-versa, which is rather convenient.

I say mostly happy, because while I am happy with the actual service, their mobile (Android) app is another story. Briefly pointing out what has been bothering me in no particular order:

  • The application is closed source (boo!)
  • The landing screen seems poorly optimized: a zoomed-out map loads an indecent number of bike markers, which cause the entire app to crawl to a halt on older hardware
